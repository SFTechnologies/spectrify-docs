## Save workspace

<hr></hr>
<br></br>
<Image
  src={`${imagePath}save_workspace_icon.svg`}
  alt='save workspace icon'
  width={65}
  height={65}
/>

Allows users to save the current state of their workspace for future use in **.spws** extension.

This includes all spectra, analysis, and other relevant settings and results. When the workspace is reopened, all of this data will be restored so you can continue your work from where you left it.
