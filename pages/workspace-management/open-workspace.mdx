## Open workspace
<hr></hr>
<br></br>
<Image src={`${imagePath}open_workspace_icon.svg`} alt="open workspace icon" width={65} height={65} />

Enables users to load a previously saved workspace file with a **.spws** extension. 

Upon loading, the workspace will restore the exact state that was saved, including all open documents, window layouts, and any other relevant settings.
